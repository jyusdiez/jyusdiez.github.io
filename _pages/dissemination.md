Conferences and Workshops
======

* I have participated in **18 conferences*, 14 international conferences, and 4 national conferences, workshops, and seminars. Over these meetings, I have been involved in 22 presentations as the lead author.
* I have also been involved as **chair** in two sessions at the European Aerosol Conference meetings of 2022 and 2023.
* I have organised a seminar as part of the SMASH MSCA COFUND seminar series. 
* Multiple workshops on instrument maintenance for the Aethalometer AE33 and the Integrating Scattering Nephelometer Aurora 4000 at TROPOS (Leipzig, Germany)
* Workshop on the use of Hysplit backtrajectories at IDAEA-CSIC (Barcelona, Spain) and Zephir (Paris, France).
* Over dozens of presentations at instituionally organized workshops and seminars (IDAEA Young Researchers, UNG seminars, SMASH seminars, etc.)
